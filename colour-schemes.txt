Color Schemes

Classic and Elegant

    Secondary Color: Gold (#FFD700)
    Accent Color: White (#FFFFFF)
    Background Color: Light Gray (#F0F0F0)

Modern and Minimalist
    Primary Color: Charcoal (#333333)
    Secondary Color: Teal (#008080)

Warm and Inviting

Fresh and Vibrant

Font Combinations

Classic and Elegant
    Headings: Playfair Display (serif)
    Body Text: Lora (serif)

Modern and Minimalist
    Headings: Montserrat (sans-serif)
    Body Text: Open Sans (sans-serif)

Warm and Inviting
    Headings: Merriweather (serif)
    Body Text: Roboto (sans-serif)

Fresh and Vibrant
    Headings: Poppins (sans-serif)
    Body Text: Lato (sans-serif)

Example Combination

For a classic and elegant look:
    Color Scheme: Navy Blue, Gold, White, Light Gray
    Fonts: Playfair Display for headings and Lora for body text

This combination gives a sophisticated and timeless feel, perfect for a bookstore that wants to convey a sense of tradition and quality.
